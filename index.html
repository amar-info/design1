We’re here to help with any issues with your Bubbel Experience

Sorry to hear that you're experiencing issues with Bubbel app. If you have not already, please try following the below steps to troubleshoot.

  • Force quit the app

  • Uninstall and reinstall Bubbel app that will upgrade you to the latest version.

  • Try restarting your device.

  • Try to use Bubbel on Wi-Fi and 3G to see if the problem has to do with your internet connection.

If you are still having trouble with the app then please fill out the information on the right to help us with investigating. Please fill out all the required fields and provide as much information as possible. You can also report the issue through Suggest Us link on the app (tap on top right corner of the app for this link).

Due to the volume of reports we receive, you may not receive a personal response - however, please be assured that we review and appreciate the reports submitted to our team through this form.
